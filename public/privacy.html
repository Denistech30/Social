Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Our Commitment

TextCraft is built with privacy as a core principle. Your text, data, and usage remain completely private and secure.

Data Collection

We collect ZERO personal data. All text formatting happens entirely in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.

  • No text content is stored on our servers
  • No personal information is collected
  • No tracking cookies are used
  • No third-party analytics beyond basic Google Analytics (page views only)

Local Storage

We use browser localStorage only to:

  • Remember your preferences (optional)
  • Track copy count for install prompt suggestion
  • Store your draft text locally (never sent to servers)

You can clear this data anytime by clearing your browser cache.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand:

  • How many people visit TextCraft
  • Which features are most popular
  • General geographic regions (country level only)

No personally identifiable information is collected.

Third-Party Services

TextCraft does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data. Everything runs client-side in your browser.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to delete or request. You have complete control and privacy by default.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Check this page periodically for updates. The "Last updated" date will reflect changes.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.
